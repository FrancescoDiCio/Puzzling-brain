Incompatible Hairstyle/Head Covering

Here are some examples of hairstyles and head coverings that can interfere with the fNIRS system. Since the cap needs to fit closely to your head for accurate readings, certain hairstyles, like extensions or braids, might make this difficult. If your hairstyle falls into one of these categories, unfortunately you cannot take part to the study. If you wear any head covering items, as long as you can remove them during the experiment, you can take part in our study!
